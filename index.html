Yiheng Xiong

I am a PhD student at DFG Training Group KEMAI, Ulm University, working with Prof. Michael Götz. Previously, I obtained my Master's degree from the Technical University of Munich and my Bachelor's degree from Nanjing University.

My current research interests lie in the intersection of computer vision and machine learning with their applications in medical imaging analysis.
